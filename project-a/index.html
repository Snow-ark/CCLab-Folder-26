[ Snow ]

Scientific Name:

Fallenis Angelas

Discovered by / at:

Floating in the sky at 12AM

Family:

Unknown

Habitat:

Modern City

Appearance:

A slightly glowing blue angel with floating wings and a golden halo with a blindfold.

Key Characteristics:

  • Sensitive to touch and sound
  • Will fly away if irritated
  • Kind
  • Radiates power from halo

What we know so far about [ Snow ]?
Mysterious entity that floats in the sky above the city. From what we have observed, it hates touch. Don't get too close to the angel! And don't ever click on it... it might run away. But don't worry. The angel will come back. Also whenever the clock is striked, the angel will radiate some sort of divine power from its halo. It is quite the mysterious event.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature
Please be very careful when interacting with this creature because it does not like to be touched and it will be triggered if you click it with your mouse and fly away. Click on the clock of the clock tower to witness the power radiate from the angel's halo!
